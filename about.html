Gregg Rossman

Gregg Rossman is an accomplished litigator with over 20 years of trial experience. As an assistant State Attorney, he has tried some of the most complicated and high profile cases in Broward County. Many of those cases have been featured in the local and national media. Gregg has been interviewed by ABC News on Good Morning America, CBS4 Miami, NBC6 Miami, WSVN7, WPLG10 Miami, and the Wall Street Journal, Sun-Sentinel and Miami Herald newspapers. Gregg has strong ties and connections in the law enforcement community. He is a second generation Miamian who has deep roots in the South Florida community.
